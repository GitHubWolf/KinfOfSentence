Well...This software was initially written for myself,but I decided to share it on the network freely so that other people could benefit from it.
Usages:
1.On the "Study" screen, slide left to get previous sentence, slide right to get the next sentence， slide up or slide down to change the class and tap to get the answer.
2.On the "Class" screen, tap to select the class and chapter.
